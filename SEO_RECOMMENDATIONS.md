## Character Count Guidelines

### Title Tags
- **Minimum:** 30 characters
- **Ideal:** 50-60 characters
- **Maximum:** 60 characters (Google truncates after ~60)

### Meta Descriptions
- **Minimum:** 120 characters
- **Ideal:** 150-160 characters
- **Maximum:** 160 characters (Google truncates after ~160)

### Why These Limits Matter
- **Mobile displays:** Show fewer characters
- **Truncation:** "..." appears when too long, losing impact
- **Click-through rate:** Complete, compelling messages perform better
- **User experience:** Users see full message in search results
